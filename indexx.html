Our Story

I started as a passionate Physics teacher in a private school, shaping young minds within the walls of a structured system. But deep down, I wanted to do more—to build something greater with a team who shared my vision.

In 2022, I took a leap of faith. I left my regular job to create an academy where learning wasn’t just about textbooks, but about growth, confidence, and real success. I wasn’t alone in this journey—I had an incredible team beside me, bringing their expertise, dedication, and belief in what we could accomplish together.

Like any entrepreneur, I faced hurdles. As a woman stepping into leadership, there were doubts, challenges, and moments that tested my resilience. But I stood firm, knowing that with the right people beside me—my team, my family, and my friends— we could push through anything.

Over the years, we’ve trained countless students, helping them achieve their academic goals and beyond. Seeing their progress, their victories, and their growing confidence is what keeps us going.

This academy is not just an institution—it’s a testament to teamwork, perseverance, and the courage to chase a dream. And we’re just getting started.

- Banupriya P , Founder of Infinity Academy
